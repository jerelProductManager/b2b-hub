Virtual Production Cleveland Institute of Art

XR Stage & Interactive Media Lab — LED Volume + ICVFX Infrastructure

The Studio designed and integrated a broadcast-grade virtual production environment for CIA's 14,297 sq ft Interactive Media Lab — giving students, faculty, and industry partners on-demand access to a fully operational LED volume and real-time render pipeline. The project required coordinating LED wall specification, camera tracking, Unreal Engine pipeline setup, and power infrastructure within a working academic building, on an education timeline.

Metropolitan Broadcast Group — Live IP Production Overhaul

A regional news network operating three markets needed to migrate from legacy SDI to a full ST 2110 IP production environment without interrupting daily live output. The Studio managed phased system design, equipment specification, and on-site commissioning across all three facilities. Result: zero broadcast interruptions across a 14-month transition window.

Post-Production

Harbor Post NY — Color Suite & Shared Storage Architecture

A boutique post house expanding from 3 to 9 seats needed a shared storage and DaVinci Resolve color pipeline that could handle 8K RAW without bottlenecks. The Studio specified a high-bandwidth NVMe-over-Fabric SAN, configured multi-user Resolve licensing, and built out two purpose-designed grading suites — from acoustic treatment to display calibration.
